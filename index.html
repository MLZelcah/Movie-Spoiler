Plot

The human race is trapped on a now dying planet. There a man named

Cooper lives along with his daughter, Murph and his son Tom. Murph begin to notice odd magnetic occurances

which alarm Cooper. Together they go to the supposed origin of the magnetism. To their surprise they

find the remnants of NASA preparing for one last mission to the stars in hopes of discovering a

suitable new home for the human race. Promising they would find a method to move the human race

the vast distance, Cooper agrees to help, having been a prepared astronaut. Cooper, along with

3 other astronauts, use a space ship to reach three suitable planets surrounding a black hole.

However the first planet they go to they lose one astronaut and due to time dialation take many years

even though it was only a few hours to them. On the next planet they find an old astronaut sent a long time ago.

While it seems suitable, the old astronaut betrays them, kills one of the astronauts, and nearly destroys their ship.

Damaged, Cooper skillfully prepare to slingshot a part of the ship to the next planet wih the last other astronaut

in hopes of finding a suitable home. However to preform this feat Cooper has to sacrifice his side of the ship,

sending him deep into the black hole. In the black hole, Cooper ship start to get hit by rocks, then at the last

minute he ejects, only he's still alive, floating. Then all asuden tons of bookcases surround him. Suddenly he see's

himself and Murph talking just before he leaves, all the while desperately attempting to communicate with the past.

Suddenly he realizes his actions were creating the odd magnetic occurances that made him aware of this whole thing.

Then he uses morse code to tell Murph the gravitational key of the black hole to finally find the solution to get

humans to a new home. Suddenly the book cases disappear and Cooper blacks out. Suddenly he awakens in a bed, in a

nice area. A doctor comes in and he askes what this is, to which he is told that Murph solved the problem and humans

are going to the new world. Cooper is overjoyed and then finds his daughter. She is now an old woman, on her death bed.

Cooper is filled with overwhelming happine and sorrow. That he finally reunited with his daughter and that he was away

from his daughter for her whole life. Then Cooper leaves, taking a spacehip to reunte with the astronaut he abandoned

in space.
